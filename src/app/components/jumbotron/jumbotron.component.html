Yu-Gi-Oh! Draft Cube Simulator

Welcome to Yu-Gi-Oh! Draft Cube Simulator, a place where you and friends can draft and build a deck from a curated prebuilt cube of cards or sealed packs. Drafted decks can be exported as a .ydk and imported into DuelingBook, EdoPro, Dueling Nexus, etc. No sign up is required to start drafting, but sign in or sign up to create your cubes and star your favorite cubes for next time.
