Yu-Gi-Oh! Draft Cube Simulator

!!! WORK IN PROGRESS !!!

Welcome to Yu-Gi-Oh! Draft Cube Simulator, a place where you and friends can draft and build a deck from a curated prebuilt cube of cards or sealed packs. Drafted decks can be exported as a .ydk file and imported into DuelingBook, EdoPro, Dueling Nexus, YGO Omega, etc. No sign up is required to start drafting, but sign in or sign up to create your cubes and star your favorite cubes for next time. This is currently a work in progress, so most of the major functionality is not currently working. Feel free to play around in the meantime, and come back soon when we're ready for some drafting fun.
